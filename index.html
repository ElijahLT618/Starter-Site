The Best Top 5 Zombie Games

Number 1 Project Zomboid

Project zomboid provides the most realistic version of surviving the Zombie Apocalypse.
From needing to read for being able understand eletrical work to doing yoga so you're character can run and climb faster.
Also with 300x300 tiled map with either vanilla or modded POIs. To top off where the game sets place is 1970's Fort Knox
In Project Zomboid your only given one life and the goal is to see what stats,
you can max out and how long you can survive with one character!!!

Diffculty:10/10

Number 2 Days Gone

Days Gone is semi realism zombie story game that has you traveling post apocalyptic Oregon in search of your girlfriend.
The game provides a terriorty based looting and shopping from vendors.
The map is 5x7 tiled preset map that as challeges and side quests hidden around the map.
In Days Gones your end goal is to finish the story so you can progress what happens in youre world
and to survive the roaming hordes that run and come in hundreds while in a grand quest to find youre chick!

Diffculty:8/10

Number 3 They Are Billions

They Are Billions is city builder set in a post apocalyptic world.
It is a RTS style game where you spawn in a mission or a sandbox with the end goal to make to the final day.
As hordes of the undead progessly get more tanky and dangerous the more days pass in game till a horde day.
It has really good mechanics when it comes to messing around in the sandbox feature.
The maps are produceral genrated when you start a sandbox but missions are preset maps with special challeges and limited resources.

Diffculty:5/10

Number 4 DeadLight

Deadlight is a parkour/platform where you use the enviroment to kill zombies and perfrom platfrom challeges while being chased.
The game takes place in a post apocalyptic Seattle in 1986 you start in the middle of the city and each stage you slowly make your way out of the city.
The goal of the game is to find youre family because youre character is a former park ranger in seek of a job when the dead start walking the streets.
It's a 2D platformer so their isnt crazy gameplay mechanics but a fun platformer.

Diffculty:7/10

Number 5 Dayz

Dayz is a open survival game focus on the overall gun play mechanics betweeen you and other online players.
The game takes place in Russia in world where the Soviet Republic took over and is in a map called Chernarus in the year 2009.
The goal of the game is to either survive as long as you can with one character or to see if you can build a base fill it up with loot.
Its a preset map that never changes unless you decided to play on a server with a map dlc.

Diffculty:4/10
