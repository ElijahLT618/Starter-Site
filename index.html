The Best Top 5 Zombie Games

Project zomboid provides the most realistic version of surviving the Zombie Apocalypse.
From needing to read for being able understand eletrical work to doing yoga so you're character can run and climb faster.
Also with 300x300 tiled map with either vanilla or modded POIs. To top off where the game sets place is 1970's Fort Knox
In Project Zomboid your only given one life and the goal is to see what stats,
you can max out and how long you can survive with one character!!!

Diffculty:10/10

Number 2 Days Gone

Days Gone is semi realism zombie story game that has you traveling post apocalyptic Oregon in search of your girlfriend.
The game provides a terriorty based looting and shopping from vendors.
The map is 5x7 tiled preset map that as challeges and side quests hidden around the map.
In Days Gones your end goal is to finish the story so you can progress what happens in youre world
and to survive the roaming hordes that run and come in hundreds while in a grand quest to find youre chick

Diffculty:6/10
